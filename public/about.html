About TimeMachine

Our story of craftsmanship and innovation

Our Heritage

Founded in 1985, TimeMachine has been at the forefront of watchmaking excellence for nearly four decades. What started as a small workshop in Switzerland has grown into an internationally recognized brand known for precision, quality, and timeless design.

Our Mission

We believe that a watch is more than just a timekeeping device. It's a statement of personal style, a symbol of achievement, and a companion for life's most precious moments. Our mission is to create timepieces that not only tell time but also tell your story.

Craftsmanship

Each TimeMachine watch is crafted with meticulous attention to detail. Our master watchmakers combine traditional techniques with modern technology to create timepieces of exceptional quality and durability.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on materials or craftsmanship, ensuring every watch meets our highest standards.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of watch design and technology while respecting tradition.

Sustainability

We're committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable manufacturing practices.
